ABOUT US

Savz is a fashion label established and envisioned by four teenagers in the early 2021. The brand has chosen a distinctive approach in creating minimalistic luxury garments in this modernistic era. We strongly believe in presenting our intended message through our products while refining high quality garments, with designing and detailing treated with infinite care. The brand is constantly progressing and learning to raise the benchmark of the brand while also experiencing new ideas and methods to serve the world with high street modernistic quintessential staples.
