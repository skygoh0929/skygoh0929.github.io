SHIPPING CONDITIONS


Under normal circumstances, you will receive your parcel within 10 working days after placing your order. You may experience slight delay for shipping due to Covid-19 Pandemic so please allow 3 to 5 extra working days. Delivery will be delayed for Sabah & Sarawak due to MCO/CMCO/EMCO, sorry for the inconvenience caused.

TRACKING INFORMATION


In order to track your order, please kindly check your email to get your tracking number or message from your registered contact number via EasyParcel Courier Service Platform.


Your order can be checked by visiting the website of the courier service.


As a precaution, please keep your receipt of your order. If the tracking number shows "tracking number invalid" or "tracking number not found", most likely the parcel status is not updated by the courier service yet. You can check again the next day.
